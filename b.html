BJP(Bharatiya Janata Party)

BJP HAS COME TO POWER FOR FOUR AT CENTER

First Term : May 1996 - May 1996

BJP under the umbrella came to power at center, for the first time on 16th May, 1996.
It was led by one of the most respected prime minister India has seen : Mr Atal Bihari Vajpayee. But,joy was short-lived, as he was unable to prove majority and resigned after 13 days.

Second Term : Mar 1998 - April 1999

In 1998 general elections, after fall of two United Front government,
BJP once again come into power forming alliance with other regional parties.
This government lasted for 13 months.
Ms Jayalalitha decided to withdraw support resulting in another general elections (NDA lost vote of confidence by just 1 vote).

Third Term : October 1999 - May 2004

In 1999 general elections, again BJP (under NDA )came into power by winning 303 seats in Lok Sabha.
This time, they managed to rule for 4.5 years and took a hasty decision to pre-pone the general elections by 6 months which they lost.
For all the three terms, Mr Vajpayee was at the helm of affairs. He retired from public life in 2006.

Fourth Term : 26 May 2014- Present

In 2014, after a decade of being ruled by UPA ( mainly congress), Indians decided that its time for a change and voted for BJP.
BJP alone was able to get 284 seats and hence past the majority cross.
This scenario happened after 30 years in Indian politics.
Mr. Narendra Modi, one of the most known face of BJP at present, became prime minister.
BJP with its allies together called NDA, is running the government and making opposition sweat.
